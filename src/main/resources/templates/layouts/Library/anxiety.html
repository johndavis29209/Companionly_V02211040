Understanding Anxiety and Managing It Effectively

Anxiety is a common emotional state characterized by feelings of unease, worry, fear, and apprehension. It can be a normal response to stressful situations, but when it becomes excessive or persistent, it can interfere with daily life and indicate an anxiety disorder.
