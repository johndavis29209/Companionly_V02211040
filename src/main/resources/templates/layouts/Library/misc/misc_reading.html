Understanding Mental Health and Managing It Effectively

Mental health disorders come in many forms, affecting thoughts, emotions, behaviors, and daily functioning. While anxiety, stress, and depression are widely recognized, there are several other common mental health conditions that impact millions of people worldwide. These disorders may vary in severity and symptoms but often require awareness, understanding, and proper management.
